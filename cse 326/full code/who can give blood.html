WHO CAN GIVE BLOOD ?

A DONOR MUST HAVE TO SATISFY ALL THE MEDICAL CONDITIONS MENTIONED BELOW:

1. BLOOD PRESSURE

The Systolic and Diastolic blood pressure should lie
within normal limits,i.e 120/80.

2. AGE

Donor Should be in the age group of 18 to 65 years.

3. WEIGHT

The Donor should not be less than 45 kilograms.

4. MALARIA

Should not have been treated for malaria in last 3 months
or 3 years if residing in endemic areas.


5. HAEMOGLOBIN

Haemoglobin should not be less than 12.5 grams


6. HEART DISEASE

Donor should NOT have any heart disease.
He must have a healthy heart with beat count normal


7. DIABETES

YES if resolved or controlled by diet or oral drug .
NO if treated with insulin injections.


8. TATTOO

should NOT have had tattoo in the last 6 months.




9. TOOTH

YES if whitening or impression
YES the day after a filling,cleaning or orthodontics
YES 3 days after extraction, surgery ,root canal or implant

10. CHILD BIRTH

A woman should have delivered 1 year ago
and stopped lactation


11. DRUGS

You cannot give blood if injected intravenously,even once.
